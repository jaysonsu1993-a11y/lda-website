# 竞品分析

## 主要竞品

### 1. Morningstar Engineering
- **网站:** https://www.morningstar.io
- **定位:** 最知名的 MIDI 控制器品牌
- **产品:** MIDI 控制器、Loop Switcher
- **特点:** 社区驱动、软件生态完善

### 2. EM Custom (欧洲)
- **网站:** https://emcustom.eu
- **定位:** 欧洲高端定制
- **产品:** MIDI Looper、Controller、Amp Switcher

### 3. One Control USA
- **网站:** https://one-control.com
- **产品:** Minimal Series MIDI Dual Stereo Loop

### 4. Bright Onion Pedals
- **网站:** https://brightonion.co.uk
- **产品:** True Bypass Loopers、Signal Routing

### 5. Buzz Electronics
- **网站:** https://www.buzzelectronics.co.uk
- **产品:** Looper、MIDI Switcher

### 6. Hotone Audio
- **网站:** https://www.hotone.com
- **产品:** Ampero Control (蓝牙 MIDI 控制器)

### 7. BOSS
- **网站:** https://www.boss.info
- **产品:** ES-8 Effects Switching System

### 8. Lehle
- **网站:** https://www.lehle.com
- **产品:** ABY Switcher with MIDI

### 9. Voodoo Lab
- **产品:** PX-8 Plus 8-loop Pedal Switcher

### 10. Singular Sound
- **产品:** MIDI Maestro
